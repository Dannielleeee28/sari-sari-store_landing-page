About DADD'S SARI-SARI STORE

Born out of the extraordinary circumstances of 2020, DADD'S SARI-SARI STORE isn't just a place to shop â€“ it's a testament to resilience, dedication, and the power of perseverance. While the world grappled with uncertainty during the pandemic, we embarked on our own ambitious journey.


From the very beginning, our path was anything but easy. We vividly remember the "super hassle" of assembling every single rack, meticulously encoding countless product barcodes into our POS system, often retrying multiple times amidst the confusion. There were moments of doubt and frustration, but each challenge only fueled our determination to bring affordable and quality groceries to our community. Simultaneously, we tirelessly connected with numerous distributors, fighting to secure the lowest possible prices so we could offer competitive retail and wholesale options to you.


What started in a period of global challenge has blossomed into a store built on hard work and a deep commitment to our customers. We poured our hearts into every detail, driven by the belief that even in tough times, essential goods should be accessible and shopping should be a reliable experience.


Today, DADD'S SARI-SARI STORE stands proud, a beacon of that initial dream. We continue to strive for excellence, constantly seeking ways to improve and serve you better. Every shelf filled and every product scanned carries the story of our humble, yet ambitious, beginnings. Thank you for being a part of our journey.
